Our concept

Do you know that with one plant we can create dozens? We propose you an economic and playful way to make your plants grow by yourself. On Plante Propagation it is possible to order a cutting of the plant of your choice for only a few euros.

What is a cutting

The cutting is a technique of reproduction of plants. By selecting only a small part of an existing plant it is possible to grow a new one by only dipping it in water.
